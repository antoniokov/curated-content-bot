### End-of-session checklist

When I say "we're done", "ship it", "finish up", or similar — follow this sequence:
1. If you haven't yet, run tests and confirm they all pass. If any fail, fix them before proceeding.
2. Update plan.md: mark the current task as completed and move it to the "Done" section at the top.
3. Stage all changes related to this session.
4. Propose a short meaningful commit message (one line, imperative mood). Show it to me and wait for my approval or edits before committing.
5. After I confirm, commit and push to the current branch without asking for further permissions.
